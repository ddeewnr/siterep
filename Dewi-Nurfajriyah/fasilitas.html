Our Facilities

Designed to support a calm, focused, and comfortable reading experience.

Quiet Reading Area

A dedicated reading space designed for focus and comfort. The atmosphere is kept calm and quiet, allowing visitors to enjoy books without distraction.

Cozy Reading Corners

Small and comfortable corners for personal reading moments, reflection, or light discussions in a relaxed setting.

Café Corner

A small café area where visitors can enjoy coffee or tea. This facility is designed to complement the reading experience without disturbing library activities.

Free Wi-Fi & Power Outlets

Reliable internet access and power outlets are available to support reading, research, and light productivity.
